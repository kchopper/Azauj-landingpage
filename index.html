Who We Are

Founded in 2015, AZAUJ is a Lahore based technological venture being run by a reliable team of well educated, proactive and understanding Pakistani professionals. It is a revolutionary service for single people seeking marriage proposals in a private, secure and responsible environment where they are not exposed to public listings and unwanted calls. The initiative was taken to connect right people with each other through IT tools after thoroughly discussing with them their requirements, and the social and cultural values they live by. It saves our clients from time and resource wastage, and physical and mental exertion which most go through while following the traditional system of matchmaking and other online matrimonial services. To know more about us, you can either visit our offices in Lahore and London or call us on the numbers given in the contact us section.

Vision

Our vision is to revolutionize traditional match making for Pakistanis by incorporating sophisticated technology tools, customized according to our social and cultural norms/needs.

Mission

We aim to become a trusted brand and a default go-to matrimonial service for millions of single Pakistanis worldwide.
